Definitions

Derivative:
The rate of change at a specific point of a function. Written as (dy/dx) which means the derivative of y with respect to x.
Limit:
As the x-values approach a,the values of f get closer and closer to y = L
Mean Value Theorem
If f is continuous on the closed interval [a,b] and differentiable on the open interval (a,b), then there exists a number c in (a,b) such that f'(c)= [f(b)-f(a)]/(b-a)
Rate of Change:
The slope of a function.
Rolle's Theorem
If a function, f, is continuous on the closed interval [a, b] and differentiable on the open interval (a, b) and f(a) = f(b), then there is at least one number c in (a,b) such that f′(x) = 0
